# Proposal Edit Karne ka Mukammal Tariqa (Beginner Guide)

Ye guide aapko batayegi ke aap is system ke zariye aik professional proposal kaise bana sakte hain aur usay kaise edit kar sakte hain.

---

## Step 1: Basic Information (Apni aur Client ki Detail)
Jab aap app kholte hain, toh aapko **Stepper Form** nazar aata hai. 

1. **Your Information**: Yahan aapka naam aur email pehle se bhara hoga (profile se).
2. **Client Information**: Yahan client ka naam aur sahi email address likhein.
   > [!IMPORTANT]
   > Client ka email sahi hona zaroori hai taake validation error na aaye.

---

## Step 2: Project Details (Project ki Tafseelat)
Agli slide par aapko project ki details deni hain:

1. **Brand Name**: Maslan "Humantek".
2. **Project Title**: Project ka naam (e.g., "E-commerce Website Development").
3. **Business Description**: Business ke bare mein thori detail likhein.
4. **Proposed Solution**: Aap kya solution de rahe hain, wo yahan likhein.

---

## Step 3: Costs aur Budget (Currency aur Paisa)
Ye step bohat aham hai kyunke yahan aap budget set karte hain.

1. **Currency Select Karein**: Aap USD, PKR, GBP, EUR, ya AED mein se koi bhi select kar sakte hain.
2. **Advance Percentage**: Aap kitne percent advance lena chahte hain (e.g., 50%).
3. **Additional Costs**: Agar koi extra charges hain toh wo bhi yahan likhein.
   - Example: Agar aap 200,000 likhenge toh ye khud hi "2 Lakh" show karega (PKR ke liye).

---

## Step 4: Unified Editor (Fine-Tuning aur Preview)
Jab aap form bhar lete hain, toh aap **Unified Editor** ka istemal kar sakte hain jo ke split-screen par hota hai.

### View Modes (3 Tarah ke Views)
- **Edit Mode**: Sirf editor panel dikhta hai
- **Split Mode**: Editor aur PDF preview dono sath mein (recommended)
- **Preview Mode**: Sirf PDF preview dikhta hai

### Page Visibility Settings
Aap kisi bhi page ko hide ya show kar sakte hain:
- **Additional Info**: On/Off toggle
- **About HT**: On/Off toggle
- **Pricing**: On/Off toggle
- **Payment Terms**: On/Off toggle

---

## Step 5: Har Page Ko Edit Karna

### ğŸ“„ Cover Page (Editor 1)
Ye proposal ka pehla page hai jo client dekhta hai.

**Kya Edit Kar Sakte Hain:**
- **Brand Name**: Company ka naam

---

### ğŸ“˜ About HT (Editor 2)
Yahan aap apni company ke bare mein batate hain.

**Features:**
- **Title**: Page ka heading (default: "About Humantek")
- **Subtitle**: Optional sub-heading
- **Text Elements**: Paragraphs add kar sakte hain
- **Image Elements**: Company ki photos upload kar sakte hain

**Kaise Add Karein:**
1. "Add Text" button se paragraph add karein
2. "Add Image" se photo upload karein
3. Har element ko edit ya delete kar sakte hain
4. Images automatically resize ho jati hain

---

### ğŸ“‹ Additional Info (Editor 3)
Ye sabse powerful editor hai - yahan aap tables, lists, aur sections add kar sakte hain.

**Kya Add Kar Sakte Hain:**

#### 1. **Sections (Title + Content)**
- Title wala section add karein
- Bullet points ya numbered lists add karein
- Sub-bullets bhi add kar sakte hain

#### 2. **Tables (2 ya 3 Columns)**
- **2 Column Table**: Deliverables ke liye (Item | Time)
- **3 Column Table**: Quotation ke liye (Item | Quantity | Cost)
- Headers edit kar sakte hain
- Rows add/delete kar sakte hain
- Number formatting (comma separated) toggle kar sakte hain

**Pro Tips:**
- Numbered lists automatic numbering karte hain (1, 2, 3...)
- Sub-bullets ke liye "Add Sub-bullet" button use karein
- Tables mein rows ko drag karke reorder kar sakte hain

---

### ğŸ’° Pricing (Editor 4)
Yahan aap apne packages aur pricing show karte hain.

**2 Tarah ke Packages:**

#### 1. **Standalone Package** (Alag se dikhta hai)
- Package title
- Subtitle/description
- Price (currency ke sath)
- Items list (checkmarks ke sath)
- Note/disclaimer

#### 2. **Grid Packages** (Side by side 2-3 packages)
- Multiple packages ek sath show hote hain
- Comparison ke liye best hai
- Har package mein title, price, items

**Features:**
- Packages automatically split hote hain agar zyada lambe hon
- Smart page breaks taake koi package beech mein na kate
- Currency symbol automatic show hota hai

---

### ğŸ“ Payment Terms (Editor 5)
Yahan payment ki conditions likhte hain.

**Kaise Use Karein:**
1. **Title**: "Payment Terms" ya koi custom title
2. **Terms List**: Har term alag line mein
   - "Add Term" button se naye terms add karein
   - Har term ko edit ya delete kar sakte hain
   - Automatic numbering (1, 2, 3...)

**Example Terms:**
- "50% advance payment required before project start"
- "Remaining 50% upon project completion"
- "Payment via bank transfer or PayPal"

> [!NOTE]
> Agar terms zyada hon toh automatically multiple pages ban jayenge.

---

## Step 6: PDF Download Karna
Jab aap editing se mutmain (satisfied) ho jayen:

1. **Generate PDF** button par click karein
2. App PDF generate karegi aur aapke server par upload bhi karegi
3. Download hone ke baad aap usay client ko bhej sakte hain

**PDF Mein Kya Hoga:**
1. Cover Page (Brand name + tagline)
2. About HT (agar visible hai)
3. Additional Info (agar visible hai)
4. Pricing (agar visible hai)
5. Payment Terms (agar visible hai)
6. Contact Page (automatic)
